About

As a freelance copywriter I produce words and develop strategies that build brand identities and create successful campaigns.

I work with companies and agencies around the world seeking a copywriter who can contribute to every stage of the creative process.

I’ve also written a book called Think Like a Copywriter, a modern guide to writing copy.

In the last year I’ve worked on campaigns for Sony, Nescafee, and Oasis, as well as several other consumer, technology, and lifestyle brands. I’ve also helped dozens of smaller companies find their voice.

How Can I Help You?

Copywriting

As a freelance copywriter I write words wherever they’re needed. I’m digitally native, with substantial traditional, integrated, and advertising experience. You give me the brief. I’ll provide the words.

Marketing and Strategy

I’ve helped companies from startups to high street stores reposition themselves, reconnect with their customers, and increase their market exposure through insightful white papers and branding reports backed up with brilliant copy.

Training and Consultancy

Using my eBook, Think Like A Copywriter, as a starting point, I provide training sessions and give talks on the art of copywriting. I’m also available as a gun-for-hire, providing expertise on an ad-hoc basis, attending pitches, seminars, or conference calls wherever my creative input is needed.
